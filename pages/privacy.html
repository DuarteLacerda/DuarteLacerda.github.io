Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 24, 2026

Summary: This Bot collects minimal data necessary for its functionality, including user IDs, message content for leveling, and temporary voice state data for music playback. We do not sell or share your data with third parties.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Automatically Collected Data

When you use the Bot, we automatically collect:

  • User IDs: Discord user IDs for leveling system and command usage
  • Server IDs: Discord server (guild) IDs where the Bot is present
  • Message Activity: Message counts for the leveling system (content is not stored)
  • Voice State: Temporary voice channel information during music playback

1.2 Command Data

When you use Bot commands:

  • Command names and parameters for functionality
  • User preferences and settings
  • Game statistics (Termo word game)

2. How We Use Your Data

We use collected data exclusively for:

  • Leveling System: Track user activity and assign experience points
  • Music Playback: Queue and play audio in voice channels
  • Game Features: Manage Termo game sessions and statistics
  • Bot Functionality: Execute commands and provide requested information
  • Service Improvement: Debug issues and improve Bot performance

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely:

  • Stored locally on our server infrastructure
  • Access restricted to Bot owner and necessary administrators
  • Data is retained only as long as necessary for Bot functionality
  • Music queue data is temporary and cleared after playback

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your data to third parties, except:

  • Discord: The Bot operates through Discord's API and is subject to Discord's Privacy Policy
  • Music Sources: YouTube or other streaming services for music playback (subject to their privacy policies)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request information about data we store about you
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Opt-Out: Stop using the Bot at any time by not interacting with it
  • Server Removal: Remove the Bot from your server, which stops data collection

To exercise these rights, contact us through our GitHub repository or Discord server.

6. Data Retention

  • Leveling Data: Retained while the Bot is in your server
  • Music Queue: Automatically cleared after playback completion
  • Game Statistics: Retained for game functionality
  • Logs: Technical logs retained for up to 30 days for debugging

7. Children's Privacy

The Bot complies with Discord's age requirements (13+ or 16+ in some regions). We do not knowingly collect data from users below Discord's minimum age requirement.

8. International Data Transfers

The Bot may be accessed from anywhere in the world. By using the Bot, you consent to the transfer of your data to our server location.

9. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. The "Last Updated" date at the top indicates when changes were last made. Continued use of the Bot after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Discord's Privacy Policy

As a Discord Bot, we operate within Discord's platform. Please review Discord's Privacy Policy for information about how Discord handles your data.

11. Contact Information

For questions, concerns, or data requests regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us through:

  • GitHub repository
  • Discord server
